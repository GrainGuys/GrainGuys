Kitchen Tile Decorating

This decorating project focused on refreshing a kitchen splashback area where the old wall finish had been stripped back and prepared. The client wanted a cleaner, brighter surface that worked with the existing cabinets, timber worktops, and appliance layout.

GrainGuys installed the blue-grey metro tiles across the worktop run, working neatly around sockets, cupboards, and the hob area. The finished splashback gives the kitchen a cleaner look and makes the worktop area easier to maintain.

The Heart of This Project

  • Kitchen splashback wall prepared
  • Blue-grey metro tiles fitted
  • Socket and worktop edges finished neatly
  • Tile lines aligned along the cabinet run
  • Existing cabinets and worktops protected
  • Clean before-to-after decorating upgrade

What Our Kitchen Fitting Includes

Our kitchen fitting service covers the installation of wall and base units, worktops, end panels, plinths, and associated finishing work. We ensure units are level, securely fixed, and aligned for a seamless look. From flat-pack kitchens to replacement unit installs, GrainGuys combines carpentry skill with practical attention to detail for a kitchen that works beautifully.

Experienced & Reliable

What Our Furniture Assembly Includes

Our furniture assembly service covers all major flat-pack brands and self-assembly units. We follow manufacturer instructions precisely, use the correct fixings, and check that doors, drawers, and moving parts operate smoothly before we leave. Whether you have a single item or a full room of furniture, GrainGuys delivers a secure, level finish without the stress.

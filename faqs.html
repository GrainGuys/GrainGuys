Furniture Assembly

We assemble flat-pack wardrobes, beds, chests of drawers, desks, shelving, and units from IKEA and other retailers. Just point us to the boxes and we will build it right the first time.

We bring our own tools, so you do not need to supply anything. Just have the flat-pack and any instructions to hand and we will take care of the rest.

Yes. We can safely fix wardrobes, shelves, and units to the wall, and mount TVs, mirrors, and brackets so everything is secure and stable.
